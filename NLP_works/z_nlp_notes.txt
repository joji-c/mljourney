NLP : natural language processing
natural - human language, language - text / words, processing - mathematical computation
its a feild of ai that enable machine to  read, understand, interpret and generate the human language
we should never change the order of a sentence when doing preprocessing

step 1: text representation :
    - lowercasing: convert all text into lower case 
    - remove the noise: removing punctuations, special characters, unwanted numbers
    - tokenization: convert each word into a token and add it into a list (sentence to words/tokens)
    - stopword removal: remove unwanted tokens like is, in, and, the... etc. from the token list to make the mathematical computation more easy
    - stemming/lemmatization: here we change word to its root form by removing tenses and supporters in it
        eg:- running -> run, largest -> large, goes - > go ......etc
        in stemming the ending of the word is cut, caughts -> caught
        in lemmatization it changes the word entirely, caught -> catch 
step 2: feature extraction (vectorization):
    TFIDF(term Frequency inverse document frequency)
step 3: model selection
step 4: model evaluation 

uses of NLP:
Sentimental analysis: Checking reviews and categorizing it as positive or negative

s1: "Earth is the third planet from the sun"
s2: "jupyter is the largest planet"
these sentences combined can be called a document

term_frequency = no. of times that token appears/total number of words in sentence
IDF(Inverse Document Frequency) = log(total sentences/no. of times document containing that token)

(log2(1)=0 power of 2 is 0 to get 1)
